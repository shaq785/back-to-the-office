How to play:

Help our Creative Director get back to the office in time to make the pitch of the year!

The year is 2021 and it’s been a doozy, to say the least. The pandemic has raged on throughout the year, but the time has come for Chuckles to fold up his sweats, set aside his sourdough starter, and return to the office. Be sure to help him boost up on the way by grabbing some coffee, masks, and vaccines! Avoid the coronavirus and the protesters or he’ll be sent back home to quarantine.

Good luck!

Use the arrow keys (or touch controls on mobile devices) to navigate through obstacles and reach the doors of the Office!

There are power-ups that will give you additional points along the way:

  • Coffee:20 PTS
  • Mask and Sanitizer:30 PTS
  • Vaccine:40 PTS

To win, you must reach 450 points to unlock the doors and enter the building.
